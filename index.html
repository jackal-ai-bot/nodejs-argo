Why Marine Life Matters

Marine life plays a vital role in maintaining the balance of our planet. Oceans produce oxygen, regulate the climate, and provide food for billions of people. However, pollution, overfishing, and climate change are putting many species at risk.

Marine Life Gallery

Sea Turtles

Sea turtles are endangered because of plastic pollution and habitat destruction.

Dolphins

Dolphins are intelligent marine mammals that need clean and healthy oceans to survive.

Coral Reefs

Coral reefs support nearly 25% of all marine species and are often called the rainforests of the sea.

How You Can Help

  • Reduce the use of single-use plastics.
  • Join beach cleanup activities.
  • Support sustainable seafood.
  • Educate others about marine conservation.
  • Donate to ocean protection organizations.
